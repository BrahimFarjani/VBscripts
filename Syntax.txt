VBScript Syntax for MsgBox:

Format: MsgBox(prompt, buttons, title)
prompt: Text displayed in the message box.
buttons: Determines the buttons and icons shown (e.g., 4+64 for Yes/No and Critical icon).
title: Title of the message box.
Message Box Styles:

vbCritical (64): Critical icon.
vbQuestion (32): Question mark icon.
vbExclamation (48): Exclamation icon.
vbInformation (64): Information icon.
Button Options: vbOKCancel (1), vbYesNo (4), vbRetryCancel (5).
